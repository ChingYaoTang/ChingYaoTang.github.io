About Me

關於我

I am currently working as a research assistant at the Institute of Astronomy and Astrophysics, Academia Sinica. My research focuses on simulating the turbulent structure formation in the primordial clouds during the early universe and aims to solve the discrepancy between simulation and observation.Besides, I have experience utilizing convolutional neural network (CNN) model to deal with gravitational wave (GW) detection and generative adversarial network (GAN) for deglitching in GW detector, which I gained during my time as an undergraduate student.

我目前在中央研究院天文及天文物理研究所擔任研究助理。我的研究專注於模擬早期宇宙原始雲中的紊流結構形成，並試著解釋模擬和觀測之間的差異。此外，我在大學期間的專題研究使用卷積神經網絡 (CNN) 模型處理重力波檢測，和使用生成對抗網絡 (GAN) 進行重力波探測器訊號的deglitching。
